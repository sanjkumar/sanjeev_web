About Me

My name is Sanjeev Kumar, I'm a web developer and designer student.

In 2012, I graduated from National College of Ireland with a Second Class Honours Diploma in Higher Diploma in Science in Web Technologies. I'm now finishing a MSc in Web Technologies at the same College, on target for a Masters Degree with Distinction.

I'm passionate about web development, I enjoy problem solving and I love taking on new challenges. I have dedicated myself to learn technologies related to Ruby on Rails, ASP.Net, which are my chosen framework to develop the majority of my projects.

Besides my enthusiasm for technology, I like movies & music and I'm also a sports lover.
